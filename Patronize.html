It’s like POS but this one can print receipts for bank transfers!

Each Pay Point device has its own bank account details attached to it (e.g. 1231123894 - Wema Bank). When money is sent to that account number via bank transfer, the Pay Point device acknowledges the payment and prints the receipt (with a merchant copy) automatically. Here’s how it works in 2 simple steps.

1. Customer sends money to the Pay Point’s account number.

2. Pay Point machine prints receipt automatically - immediately.

3. The money is settled into your wallet instantly.
